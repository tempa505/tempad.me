Tempa Dorji

I'm a computer science student and creative builder drawn to the space where logic meets art. I enjoy turning ideas into experiences, whether that's through code, design, or storytelling. My work is inspired by simplicity, culture, and emotion, with a constant curiosity for how technology can feel more human.

Education & Background

I'm a Computer Science major with a Data Science minor, and a proud alumna of UWC Red Cross Nordic in Norway, where I learned that fjords are beautiful, but winter darkness is very real.

I come from a small village in Bhutan, where the pace of life moves with the seasons, prayer flags flutter in mountain winds, and the internet... well, let's just say it arrives fashionably late. Growing up there, technology felt like something from another world, distant, almost mythical. But that contrast is what fuels me now: bridging the quiet wisdom of my roots with the buzzing possibilities of the digital age.

It's a strange and wonderful thing to come from a place where community is everything and connectivity meant walking to your neighbor's house, and now to be building in a world where a line of code can reach millions.
